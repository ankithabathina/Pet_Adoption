About Vishnu

Vishnu Vardhan is our dedicated Adoption Coordinator, connecting pets with loving families. He ensures every adoption process is smooth, transparent, and safe for both pets and adopters.

With deep knowledge of animal behavior and welfare, Vishnu provides guidance, support, and follow-ups to guarantee happy, lifelong matches.

Key Responsibilities

Adoption Screening

Evaluates applications to ensure pets go to suitable homes.

Meet & Greet Coordination

Organizes safe sessions for adopters to meet their prospective pets.

Home Visits

Conducts home checks to confirm safe and comfortable living spaces.

Follow-ups & Support

Provides guidance after adoption to ensure smooth transitions and pet welfare.
